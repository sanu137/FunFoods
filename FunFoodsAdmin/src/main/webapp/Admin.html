Restaurant Rules and Regulations

Welcome to Fun Foods! To maintain a high standard of service and ensure a positive dining experience for both our customers and staff, we have established the following rules and regulations:

  • Cleanliness and Hygiene: All employees must adhere to strict personal hygiene standards. Workstations, dining areas, and restrooms must be cleaned and sanitized regularly to prevent contamination.
  • Customer Service Excellence: Treat every customer with respect and courtesy. Promptly address their needs, feedback, or complaints. Customer satisfaction is our priority.
  • Food Safety and Handling: Proper food storage, preparation, and handling practices must be followed at all times. Ensure all food items meet safety and quality standards.
  • Workplace Conduct: Employees are expected to maintain professionalism, punctuality, and teamwork. Disrespect, harassment, or any form of misconduct will not be tolerated.
  • Ordering and Billing: Ensure all orders are entered accurately into the system. Customers should receive clear, accurate bills, and payments can be made through digital or cash methods.
  • Employee Uniform: All staff must wear clean uniforms and appropriate safety gear such as gloves and hairnets during shifts. Appearance must be neat at all times.
  • Workplace Safety: Adhere to safety guidelines to prevent accidents. Report any hazards immediately, and ensure the use of appropriate equipment for kitchen and service tasks.
  • Allergen Awareness: Clearly identify and label any potential allergens in food items. Employees should be well-informed to assist customers with dietary restrictions or allergies.
  • Time Management: Staff should respect their scheduled shifts and break times. Punctuality is critical to ensuring smooth service and reducing wait times for customers.
  • Waste Reduction: Actively engage in reducing food and material waste. Implement recycling practices and proper disposal of non-recyclable materials to minimize environmental impact.
  • Reservation and Table Management: Ensure that reservations are handled efficiently. Tables should be cleaned and prepared for the next customer immediately after use.
  • Alcohol and Age Restrictions: If alcohol is served, proper identification must be checked for customers of legal drinking age. Alcohol should not be served to minors.
  • Fire Safety: Fire exits must always be accessible. Employees should be trained in using fire extinguishers and follow fire safety protocols.
  • Smoking Policy: Smoking is prohibited inside the restaurant premises. Designated smoking areas should be clearly marked outside.
  • Mobile Devices: Employees should not use mobile phones during work hours unless necessary for work purposes. Customer-facing staff should focus on providing uninterrupted service.
  • Confidentiality: Customer information, including payment details, must be handled with confidentiality and should not be shared with unauthorized personnel.
  • Discounts and Promotions: Any promotions or discounts offered must be applied correctly. Staff should inform customers about ongoing deals and loyalty programs.
